Our Facilities

High-Speed Internet

At Amritsar Group of Colleges (AGC), students benefit from a high-speed, campus-wide internet facility that ensures seamless connectivity for academic, research, and personal use. The entire campus, including classrooms, laboratories, hostels, library, and common areas, is equipped with 24/7 Wi-Fi access, allowing students and faculty to stay connected at all times. With a dedicated high-bandwidth leased line, AGC supports smooth online learning, research activities, video conferencing, and access to digital resources without interruption.The network is secured with advanced firewalls and authentication systems to provide a safe and reliable browsing experience, making AGC a truly digitally enabled campus that empowers students to thrive in a technology-driven world.

E-Classrooms

The E-Classrooms at Amritsar Group of Colleges (AGC) are designed to integrate technology with traditional learning, creating an interactive and engaging academic environment. Equipped with smart boards, digital projectors, high-speed internet, and audio-visual aids, these classrooms enhance the teaching-learning process by making lectures more dynamic and accessible. Faculty members utilize multimedia presentations, virtual simulations, and online resources to deliver complex concepts with clarity. The e-classrooms also support live streaming, recorded lectures, and remote learning, allowing students to stay connected with their coursework anytime, anywhere.

Library Access

The library at Amritsar Group of Colleges (AGC) serves as a cornerstone of academic excellence, offering a vast collection of books, journals, research papers, and digital resources across various disciplines. Designed to provide a peaceful and productive environment, the library features spacious reading areas, high-speed internet access, and digital study zones to support both individual and group learning. With access to online databases, e-books, and scholarly articles, students and faculty can stay updated with the latest developments in their fields.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria at Amritsar Group of Colleges (AGC) offers a vibrant and hygienic space where students, faculty, and staff can relax and enjoy a variety of delicious and nutritious meals. With a menu that includes a range of affordable and healthy food options, from traditional Indian cuisine to quick snacks and beverages, the cafeteria caters to diverse tastes while maintaining high standards of cleanliness and quality. The spacious seating area provides a comfortable atmosphere for students to unwind between classes, hold informal discussions, or simply recharge during a busy day.
